~NASHVILLE HOT SAUCE~
source: unknown
servings: ~2/3c?

INGREDIENTS
-1/2c vegan butter 
-3tbsp brown sugar
-1tbsp cayenne powder
-2tsp garlic powder
-1tsp onion powder
-2tsp paprika (smoked if possible, otherwise add a drop of liquid smoke at this time)
-2tbsp white vinegar
-salt & pepper, to taste

PROCES S
Melt vegan butter in a small pot on medium-low heat, and whisk in the rest of the ingredients until combined and beginning to simmer. Let cool for a few minutes, and serve or refrigerate.
