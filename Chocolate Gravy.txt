Chocolate Gravy
Recipe: Modified & halved from HouseOfNashEats.com

INGREDIENTS
-1/2c sugar
-1/4c cocoa powder
-1.5tbsp AP flour
-pinch of salt
-1c plant milk
-1.5tbsp vegan butter or coconut oil
-1/2tsp vanilla

PROCESS
In a saucepan, whisk together the dry ingredients until there are no lumps. Whisk in the milk and cook over medium heat, stirring frequently until thickened, or for about 7-10mins. Remove from heat and stir in butter/oil and vanilla until combined. Serve over biscuits or whatever you like. Chills into chocolate pudding!
