~CHICKPEA TUNA SALAD~
source: liveeatlearn.com, modifications and notes by me
servings: 4 sandwiches

INGREDIENTS

-1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
-1/4c red onion, diced (other onions also okay)
-1/4c celery, diced
-1tsp small capers
-2 small pickles, diced
-1/4c vegan mayo
-1tsp dijon mustard
-1tsp vinegar (white preferred, but white wine, rice, red wine all good)
-1tsp pickle juice
-Salt & pepper, to taste

PROCESS
Mash chickpeas in a large mixing bowl. Dump everything else on top of it. Stir until  combined. Add to sandwich!
