SWEET & SOUR SAUCE
Source: Daringgourmet.com
Servings: ~1.5c sauce

INGREDIENTS

-1c canned pineapple juice
-3/4c brown sugar, packed
-1/3c rice vinegar
-3tbsp ketchup
-2tbsp soy sauce
-1.5tbsp corn starch

PROCESS
Whisk all ingredients together in a medium saucepan, and bring to a boil on medium-high heat. Reduce to a simmer for another minute or until thickened. Let cool completely before transferring to a container with an airtight lid. Keeps for 2-3 weeks in the fridge.
