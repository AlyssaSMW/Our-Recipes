Roasted Garlic Stuffing 9x13
Recipe: Modified from FromMyBowl.com

INGREDIENTS
-12c oven-dried bread (250F at 20mins approx), torn or cut into small pieces (aka 1 whole large french roll)
-5tbsp vegan butter/5tbsp olive oil
-1/2 yellow onion, finely diced
-2 carrots, peeled and finely diced
-3tbsp dehydrated onion, rehydrated
-4 cloves of garlic, minced
-1tbsp nutritional yeast
-2tsp powdered sage
-1tsp each of dried rosemary & thyme, pulverized to a powder in mortar & pestle
-1tsp black pepper
-2tsp salt
-2tbsp Sherry
-2c roasted garlic broth (better than bouillon + warm water, stirred until fully combined)

PROCESS
Preheat the oven to 350F and grease a 9x13 baking dish with olive oil and set aside. Melt the butter in a large pan and add onions and carrots. Saute on medium heat for 5-6mins, or until onion starts to brown. Add garlic & herbs, and stir. Saute until fragrant. Add the sherry and stir. Turn off the heat and add all your bread, stirring to coat evenly. Add the garlic broth and stir to coat. Transfer to a baking dish and cover with foil. Bake for 20mins covered, and 20mins uncovered.
