~VEGAN KEWPIE MAYO~
source: theedgyveg.com
-servings: 1c mayo

INGREDIENTS
-2tbsp + 1tsp rice vinegar (seasoned preferred)
-1/4tsp MSG
-Pinch turmeric
-1tbsp white sugar
-1c vegan mayo

PROCESS
RECOMMENDED: Simmer vinegar in small saucepan over high heat, and then lower the heat to medium to keep it simmering for 3-5mins until reduced to 1tbsp, and then do the following. 
WHAT I DID: Place vinegar and all ingredients except mayo into a bowl, and whisk to combine. Add mayo and whisk until smooth. Refrigerate until chilled.
