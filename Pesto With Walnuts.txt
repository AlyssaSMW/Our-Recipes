~CLASSIC WALNUT PESTO~
source: minimalistbaker.com

-2 Cups fresh basil, minus stems
-3tbsp walnuts (or any nut/seed)
-3 cloves garlic
-2tbsp lemon juice
-3tbsp nutritional yeast or vegan parmesan
-1/2tsp crushed red pepper, optional, for spicy
-1/4tsp salt
-3tbsp EVOO
-3-6tbsp water, as needed for texture

Blend all ingredients into food processor, adding oil and water gradually.
