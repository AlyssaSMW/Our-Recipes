~CORN BREAD~
source: noracooks.com
servings: 9

INGREDIENTS
-1 1/4c AP flour
-1c corn meal
-2/3c sugar
-1tsp salt
-1tbsp baking powder
-1 1/4c vegan milk
-1/3c oil (canola, olive, veggie)
-Any mix-ins you like (cheese, jalapenos, etc)

PROCESS
Preheat oven to 400F and grease an 8x8 glass baking dish. In a large mixing bowl, combine the dry ingredients and stir. Add in the wet ingredients and mix-ins, and stir until there are no 
dry pockets of flour. Pour batter into pan, and bake for 20-25mins or until a toothpick inserted in the middle is clean. Cool and store in baking dish!
