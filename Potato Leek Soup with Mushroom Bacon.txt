POTATO LEEK SOUP WITH MUSHROOM BACON
Adapted from: veganhuggs.com
Servings: 8-10

INGREDIENTS

MUSHROOM BACON
-6 large mushrooms, washed and chopped to desired size
-1/4c bacon marinade (1tbsp olive oil, 2tbsp soy sauce, 1.5tbsp maple syrup, 1tsp liquid smoke, 1tsp garlic powder, 1tsp smoked paprika, 1/4tsp cumin, black pepper to taste)

PROCESS
Dry fry mushrooms on medium heat with a sprinkle of salt until they release their liquid, gain some color, and shrink a bit. Turn the heat down to medium-low and add marinade. Stir to coat mushrooms fully. Cook on low heat for 10-20mins, or until marinade has thickened into a glaze and mushrooms have absorbed flavor. Add soy sauce, smoked paprika, and/or sugar to taste. Set aside.

SOUP
-1.5tbsp olive oil
-1tbsp vegan butter
-1 small onion, diced
-2-3 large leeks, thinly sliced and well cleaned
-5 medium russet potatoes, roughly chopped
-4 cloves of garlic, minced
-2tsp salt, more to taste later
-2tsp ground pepper
-1.5tsp dried thyme
-1tsp dried rosemary
-1/2tsp dried sage
-6-8c water, or enough to cover veggies and then a little more
-1.5tbsp roasted veggie bouillon
-2tsp vegan chicken bouillon
-2 bay leaves
-1 can coconut milk
-Juice of 1/2 a lemon
-Sliced green onion for garnish

PROCESS
In your largest & deepest pot, melt the olive oil and butter on medium-high heat. Add the onion and leeks and saute for 5-6mins or until soft. Add the garlic and herbs, and saute another minute. Add the potatoes, mix to coat, and saute another 2-3mins. Add water and bouillons, stir to dissolve, and set temp to high to bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer for 20mins or until potatoes break apart easily. Turn off heat, remove bay leaves, and add can of coconut milk. Stir to combine. Immersion blend until fully creamy, add lemon juice, and add more salt to taste if needed. Serve topped with sliced green onion and 6-8 pieces of mushroom bacon. 
Cool for 1-2 hours, transfer to cambro, and place in fridge when no longer hot.
